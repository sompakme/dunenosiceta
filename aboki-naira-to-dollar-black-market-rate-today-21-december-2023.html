How much is a dollar to Naira today in the black market?

Dollar to naira exchange rate today black market (Aboki dollar rate):

On Thursday, the 21st of December 2023, the Lagos Parallel Market, also known as the Black Market, reported an exchange rate of ₦1,200 to a dollar for buying and ₦1,205 for selling. These figures are based on information obtained from Bureau De Change (BDC) sources.
