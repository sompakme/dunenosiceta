Royal Rumble Spoiler Leak Making The Rounds Is Not Legit

September 21, 2024
blog

WWE’s Royal Rumble event is set to take over Tropicana Field on January 27th, and Ringside News is on every development as we approach the massive event. There is a new “leak” floating around, but we have to shut it down.

A new Royal Rumble “leak” is now going around. This includes a full card and the Royal Rumble match “spoilers” for the match’s plan. It looks like an exciting event on paper, but it’s too early for that sort of thing to be a reality internally in WWE.

Ringside News can confirm that this is a bogus Royal Rumble rundown. We have no idea who started this rumor, but it is making the rounds in a big way.

The Royal Rumble match’s winner is decided, but they have not put the match on paper like this. It is an obvious fake for anyone who knows that this kind of thing surfaces every year around Royal Rumble season, and they are never legit.

The Royal Rumble card on this paper looks like what most expect. There could be some similarities to this “leak” and the actual card. Kevin Owens is expected to face Logan Paul, after all. That being said, we cannot back this up as factual at all.
