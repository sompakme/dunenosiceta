Reegan Bucket Bag

The first bag I ever received as a child was a bucket bag; my mom said it acted as my portable toy box. And now, as an adult, I find myself still gravitating toward this spacious tote style. So when I saw Kate Spade put its Reegan Bucket Bag on sale in this fall red hue, it was an immediate add to cart (it is the color of the season, after all). 

The bag also comes in black, white, and pink and features a logo-plated magnetic flap closure. It has one interior slip pocket that’s perfect for quickly accessing items like keys and phones and a concealed zipper pocket for important keepsakes. The leather bucket bag is fully lined with a fixed shoulder strap and a removable crossbody strap.

Pastry Shop Bracelet

Everyone has a specific type of jewelry they indulge in; for me, it’s bracelets — more specifically, charm bracelets. I’ve been gifted several charm bracelet styles throughout my life, and each commemorates a special memory — not to mention they also make great conversation pieces when clamoring around on your wrist. So, it’s a no-brainer that the Pastry Shop Bracelet is on my must-have list from the sale.

The gold-plated chain bracelet has eight fixed charms, including pearls, croissants, cakes, cherries, and other treats. It also has an adjustable lobster claw closure for easy on and off and an accompanying dust bag to securely store your bracelet when not in use.

Carey Zip-Top Crossbody Bag

The Carey Zip-Top Crossbody’s intricate details sold me on this bag. I imagine it pairing nicely with a chic button-down dress or elevating the simplest T-shirt and jeans. 

The crescent-shaped bag comes in green, black, white, and pink and features a fixed gold chain shoulder strap and a removable, adjustable crossbody strap. It has a zipper closure atop the main compartment, a quilted outer shell, and a fully lined interior with two slip pockets to hold your phone, keys, wallet, and other necessities.

Sailor’s Knot Stud Earrings

You can never go wrong with a classic earring like the Sailor’s Knot Studs for everyday wear. They’re dainty, unique, and my favorite thing: gold. The simplistic knotted earrings feature a push-back design for easy on and off, a gold-plated finish, and a Kate Spade dust bag for safe storing.
