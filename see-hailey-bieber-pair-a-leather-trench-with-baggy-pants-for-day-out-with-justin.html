See Hailey Bieber Pair a Leather Trench With Baggy Pants for Day Out With Justin

July 04, 2024
blog

Hailey Bieber elevated her latest casual look with a statement coat.

The model and Rhode founder was spotted heading to the movies in L.A. yesterday with husband Justin Bieber and friend Lori Harvey. For the low-key outing, Bieber wore an oversize leather trench coat, pairing the floor-sweeping piece with an ab-baring, gray cropped tee and baggy navy drawstring bottoms. She completed the look with a pair of black-and-white shoes, large gold earrings, and thin square sunglasses.

Meanwhile, the “Yummy” singer cut a casual figure in a light gray hoodie, a white T-shirt, tan trousers, and white slip-on shoes from his Drew House label. He topped off the outfit with a white baseball cap worn backward.

Rachpoot/Bauer-Griffin//Getty Images

As for Harvey, the model coordinated with Hailey in her own leather-and-cargos combination, pairing a traditional moto jacket with a black cropped tee, black pants, fuzzy black boots, and a white clutch.

Rachpoot/Bauer-Griffin//Getty Images

Hailey recently returned home to L.A. after several weeks of traveling, including the U.K. launch for Rhode and a quick European getaway, where she was joined by her husband and close friends. She shared photos of the fashion-filled getaway with fans on Instagram earlier this week, including one set of pics where she posed while layering a wet tee over a berry-toned bikini, and another where she paired a red-and-white take on the striped sailor shirt with a denim miniskirt.

The model previously opened up about her attitude when it comes to curating her own personal sense of style during an interview for Harper’s Bazaar’s September 2022 cover story.

“I get photographed so much that I feel like sometimes I put pressure on myself,” she said. “Even if I’m just throwing on jeans and a T-shirt, I want it to be a dope pair of jeans and a great T-shirt!”
