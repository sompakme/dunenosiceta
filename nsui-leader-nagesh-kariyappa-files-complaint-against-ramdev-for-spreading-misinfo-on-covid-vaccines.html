NSUI Leader Nagesh Kariyappa Files Complaint Against Ramdev For Spreading Misinfo On COVID Vaccines

July 11, 2024
blog

Mysuru – NSUI student leader Nagesh Kariyappa has filed a complaint against Baba Ramdev for misguiding people, spreading fear against vaccines, and targeting the doctors. Now, NSUI has jumped into the COVID Vaccine Allopathy controversy and is targeting Baba Ramdev, for his remarks on the allopathic doctors, and also his comments on the treatment of COVID-19 and the vaccination drive that the government is doing.

So, NSUI student leader Nagesh Kariyappa has filed a complaint against Baba Ramdev and he says that Baba Ramdev is misguiding people and spreading fear amongst the people during these times of COVID-19. How we had seen earlier how the IMA also vehemently opposed what Baba Ramdev had said and the Yoga guru himself had time and again said that Ayurveda and Yoga is something that is going to help treat COVID and not allopathic, That’s what he claimed.

As of now, much to the disappointment and anger of the medical fraternity. IMA Uttrakhand had also demanded 1000 crore rupees compensation from Baba Ramdev should he not take his statements back.

If you see yesterday also that the doctors in several states were protesting even we got our visuals of doctors who are doing surgery wearing a black armband and protesting against Ramdev. And the major point that they have put out is that it’s not allopathy Vs Ayurveda, but it’s rather on the comments on baseless comments that have been made by Baba Ramdev regarding the doctors who have been on the front line.

And now the NSUI student leader Nagesh Kariyappa had filed a complaint against Ramdev for misguiding people and spreading fear against vaccines and targeting doctors. And now the fight is intensified were yesterday also the team of doctors put out that they will not stop and they will continue with their protest until and unless there is strict action taken against Ramdev.

Their main point was that Baba Ramdev is not above the law. And why the central government and the health minister are providing aid to Ramdev and not taking action because as we know yesterday, we had lost around 594 doctors across the country and 107 doctors in the National capital Delhi. So they have been on the front line warriors there have been cases of doctors who have lost their both of parents due to COVID.

And things are very critical right now and a very unfortunate that these doctors who have been the frontline warriors have now been had to protest like this against that Ramdev, who had made such a baseless comment regarding the doctors and clearly it is intensified and now the opposition parties have also decided to pitch in and also put in complaints against Baba Ramdev regarding the entire matter.
