Adesua Etomis mom celebrates 18 years as a pastor

August 02, 2024
blog

- Actress Adesua Etomi's mother has asked people to praise and thank God for his blessings in her life

- The actress' mother, Eniiwaju Etomi, made the request while celebrating 18 years of being a pastor

- Eniiwaju said she is grateful for the years of ministry work

Popular Nollywood actress Adesua Etomi-Wellington's mother, Eniiwaju Etomi, has celebrated God's blessings in her life. The actress' mother shared a post on the photo-sharing platform, Instagram, to give thanks to God.

In the post shared on her Instagram page, Eniiwaju asked her followers to help her thank God for all the blessings he bestowed upon her. She prayed that God will richly bless her followers and their descendants as they do so for her.

The actress' mother made the request to her followers to celebrate her years of work as a pastor. She stated that she has been a pastor for 18 years.
