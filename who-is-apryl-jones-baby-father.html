REALITY television personality, Apryl Jones, is recognizable to audiences for her appearances on VH1's Love & Hip Hop franchise.

Since finding fame on Love & Hip Hop, television fans are curious to know more about Apryl's romantic past and her personal life.

Who is Apryl Jones' baby father?

Apryl Jones came on board the Love & Hip Hop franchise for the first season of their Hollywood edition, which premiered in September of 2014.

Throughout her time on the show, two of her relationships were chronicled.

One of those relationships was with her former flame and the father of her two children, Omarion - the lead singer of music group B2K.

Another one of Apryl's romances featured on the program included Lil' Fizz, another member of B2K.

Is Apryl Jones married?

From their former relationship together, Apryl Jones and Omarion have two children - one son and one daughter.

After a number of years together, the two broke up in July of 2016 - four months after the birth of their daughter.

Omarion said on Instagram of the breakup: "After this, I will not speak on or respond to any questions or comments on the matter."

The singer added: "I thank everyone in advance for respecting my wishes to keep my family life private."

Apryl struck up a romance with Omarion's B2K bandmate, Lil' Fizz, in late 2019 - it fizzled out in 2020.

Throughout 2021, it was reported that Apryl and Dr. Dre entered a romance together.

In February of 2022, reports surfaced of the reality television star and Taye Diggs allegedly beginning a relationship.

What are Apryl's and Omarion's net worth?

According to Celebrity Net Worth, Apryl Jones has a fortune of $1 million.

Omarion garnered media attention for his music career - with the music group B2K, as well as his solo releases.

Celebrity Net Worth lists the singer's riches at an estimated $8 million.
