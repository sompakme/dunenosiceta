What is causing my wiper blades to judder?

1) The water repellent on the windshield is beginning to wear away. 2) Each time they pass over the surface, the wipers remove a little amount of coating. As a result, the repellant is applied unevenly over the whole surface. Accordingly, when the wipers run over the surfaces with less product, the wiper blades get “gripped” by those surfaces. The wipers judder or skip with each pass as a result of this.
