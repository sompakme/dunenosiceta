FAQs

Does Sam Edelman offer free shipping?

Yes, Sam Edelman offers a free standard shipping option to its US customers. This will be automatically applied to your order during checkout so you won't miss out. 2-day and next-day shipping options are available for those requiring quicker delivery although there will be a charge that will be calculated at checkout.

Does Sam Edelman ever have sales?

Sam Edelman has a regular sale page which is updated at different times of the year. You can shop up to 70% off selected footwear by shopping the sale. Be sure to browse the sale during seasonal events too, such as Black Friday and Cyber Monday. Previous Black Friday sales had 30% off the entire Sam Edelman website with a specific promo code. 

Where can I find a Sam Edelman 10% first order promo code?

You can spend less on your first order by signing up for the official brand newsletter. Simply scroll to the bottom of their website and you'll find a sign-up box where you can enter your email address. Once you've signed up, you will receive your 10% off Sam Edelman coupon code. 

Is there a Sam Edelman student discount?

Yes. Students can save 15% off their online orders at Sam Edelman. Simply log into Student Beans to confirm your educational institution. You’ll then be able to see your code to save on your order. 

Does Sam Edelman have a refer-a-friend program?

Your friends and family can love Sam Edelman as much as you with their refer-a-friend program. When you share your referral link with your friends, they will receive a 15% Sam Edelman coupon to use on their first purchase. Once their order has gone through, you'll receive your own 15% code to save on your next order. 

What is the Sam Edelman returns policy?

The Sam Edelman returns policy states that returns are accepted up to 30 days after the date of delivery for a refund or exchange. All returned items should be in an unused and unworn condition with the original tags and packaging intact.
