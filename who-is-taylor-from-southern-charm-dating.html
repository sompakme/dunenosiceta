Who is Taylor From Southern Charm Dating?

August 08, 2024
blog

Taylor Ann Green, one of the latest additions to the Southern Charm cast, a popular Bravo reality show centered around the lives of Charleston, South Carolina’s social elite. She made her debut in the eighth season of the show as the girlfriend of Shep Rose, one of the original and most well-known cast members. Unfortunately, their relationship was short-lived, as Taylor announced their breakup in July 2022. Since then, Taylor has moved on to a new romantic interest, someone also connected to the Southern Charm world. Here’s the scoop on Taylor’s new beau and how they crossed paths.

Who is Taylor Ann Green?

Taylor Ann Green, a 28-year-old model and actress, originally hails from Charlotte, North Carolina. She holds a degree in communications from the University of North Carolina at Charlotte. Taylor has graced commercials, music videos, and television series, with appearances in shows like The Vampire Diaries and Drop Dead Diva. She also runs a lifestyle blog, The Taylored Life, where she shares insights on fashion, beauty, travel, and wellness.

Taylor initially crossed paths with Shep Rose through mutual friends in 2020, and their romantic journey began. She charmed both fans and fellow cast members with her warm personality and down-to-earth demeanor when she joined Southern Charm in its eighth season.

Her bond with Shep’s dog, affectionately nicknamed Lil Craig, was also a notable highlight. However, their love story came to an end after two years, and Taylor publicly confirmed their breakup via Instagram in July 2022, expressing mutual respect and friendship.

Who is Taylor Ann Green’s new boyfriend?

Taylor Ann Green has a new boyfriend named Gaston, who happens to be within the Southern Charm circle. According to Us Weekly, Gaston is the current roommate of TJ Dinch, a bartender at Leva Bonaparte’s Republic Garden & Lounge, a locale featured in the Southern Charm spinoff, Southern Hospitality. TJ is also friends with Naomie Olindo’s longtime confidant, Wilson Filogamo, who appeared on Southern Charm.

Taylor revealed that she and Gaston began talking at the end of July 2022, leading to their blossoming romance. She described him as “incredible” and praised his kindness and respect. She shared that her family approves of Gaston, underlining his family-oriented nature.

In her Instagram stories, Taylor offered more insights into Gaston. She mentioned his Argentinean origins and his involvement in real estate development. Images and videos they shared together on social media reflected their evident chemistry and affection.

How do Taylor Ann Green and Gaston feel about each other?

Taylor Ann Green and Gaston appear to be deeply in love and content in their new relationship. Taylor shared her happiness and confidence in her connection with Gaston, emphasizing the importance of finding a best friend in a partner with whom she can share laughter and memorable experiences. She expressed a desire for each day to be a gift shared with someone special.

Gaston reciprocated his feelings on his Instagram stories, posting a photo of them sharing a kiss along with a heartfelt message, “You make me happy.” He also posted a video of them dancing together, adding, “I love you.”

What does Shep Rose think of Taylor Ann Green’s new boyfriend?

Shep Rose has not publicly commented on Taylor Ann Green’s new relationship. However, he has previously expressed his well-wishes and maintained that they remain friends. In August 2022, he told Us Weekly that he was genuinely happy for Taylor and held no hard feelings. He stated that they were still buddies and that he was focused on self-improvement rather than dating.

Nonetheless, some fans speculated that Shep might harbor some jealousy regarding Taylor’s new relationship. This speculation arose following a cryptic tweet he posted on October 11, 2023, the same day Taylor introduced her new boyfriend on Instagram. In the tweet, Shep wrote, “I’m sorry but I can’t help but laugh at some things,” without specifying the subject, leaving some fans to wonder if it related to Taylor.

Conclusion

In conclusion, Taylor Ann Green is now in a relationship with Gaston, someone with connections to the Southern Charm world. Their romance appears to be flourishing, and Shep Rose, her previous boyfriend, has wished her well as they continue to maintain their friendship. The couple is happily documenting their journey on social media for their fans to follow.
