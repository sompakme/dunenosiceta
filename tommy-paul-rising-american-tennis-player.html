Tommy Paul: Thomas John “Tommy” Paul was born on the 17th of May in the year 1997. He is an American professional tennis player. Paul managed to earn the career-high ATP singles ranking of world No. 33. He achieved this title on the 11th of April 2022. He has a doubles ranking of No. 139. He achieved this on the 9th of May 2022. He went on to win his maiden ATP title at the 2021 Stockholm Open.

Playing style

Paul has a strong attacking forehand and footwork with great grip along the baseline. Paul is under the coaching of Brad Stine since 2020.
