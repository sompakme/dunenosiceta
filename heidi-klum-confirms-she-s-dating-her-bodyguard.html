Heidi Klum confirms she's dating her bodyguard

Heidi Klum has admitted that she's dating her bodyguard Martin Kirsten. Photo / AP

Heidi Klum has confirmed she is dating her bodyguard.

The 39-year-old model - who was accused by estranged husband Seal of cheating on him with Martin Kirsten, before claiming his quotes were taken out of context - admits she is in the early stages of getting to know him, but claims she trusts him because of their four year working relationship.

Speaking on Katie Couric's show Katie, she said: "I don't even know if I could call it [a relationship], you know, it has just started. I don't know, I don't know where it is going to go.

"Yes, yes, I've known him for four years and he's been with our family for four years. He's cared for our entire family, mostly for our four children. He's helped us tremendously, he's helped our children. I trust him with my children.
