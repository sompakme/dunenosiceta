Coolest New Hair Accessories - Best Runway and Red Carpet Headbands and Hair Clips

September 15, 2024
blog

Every product on this page was chosen by a Harper's BAZAAR editor. We may earn commission on some of the items you choose to buy.

1

Tibi

The sleek, low ponytails at the Tibi show were looped and tied with belts. It's a look we never thought we'd absolutely love.

2

Belt Barrette

The easiest hairstyle ever: pull your hair back into a loose half-up style, and secure it with this belt-inspired barrette. 

Asos Buckle Hair Barrette, $9.67, asos.com

3

Erdem

A black ribbon is one of the most versatile (and inexpensive) hair accessories. We love how this one acts as both a headband and a decorative bow, framing the intricate braided bun at the Erdem spring 2017 show.

4

Flower Power

Even the messiest bun or braid can look purposeful when decorated with one (or two, or five) of these flower pins.

Emi Jay Leather Camellias, $35 each, emijay.com

5

Fendi

More was more at the spring 2017 Fendi show, where hairstylist Sam McKnight and his team took leftover baubles from the collection and glued them on to hair pins. 

6

Chic Circle

Slick and graphic, this clasp looks just as great on fuzzy waves as it does on sleek and shiny hair.

Jen Atkin X Chloe + Isabel Oversized Circle Clip, $18, chloeandisabel.com

7

Chanel

@SamMcKnight1 on Instagram

You're not going to want to lose these hair ties: the colorful charm elastics used to secure hair into side ponytails at the spring Chanel show.

8

So Fluffy

This elastic is more whimsical than cutesy when wrapped around a sleek low ponytail.

Rosantica Bianca feather and pearl hair tie, $155, net-a-porter.com

9

Queen Bee

Having a bee in your hair never looked so good.

Jennifer Behr bee gold tone hair tie, $135, net-a-porter.com

10

Bottega Veneta

The jeweled barrettes at Bottega Veneta were place directly behind the ear, which made them seem more like a continuation of the earrings than an additional glitzy accoutrement.

11

Crystal Topper

If you're looking for sparkle, look no further. Pearls, stars, Swarovski crystals: this barrette has it all.

Alexander McQueen Silver-plated, Swarovski crystal, and pearl hair slide, $395, net-a-porter.com

12

Marchesa

Whether slicked-back or naturally curly, the hair at Marchesa was decorated with starbust-shaped pins placed just above the ear.

13

Prettiest Pin

There's no wrong way to wear this oversized comb, just as long as it feels secure and the teeth are totally hidden.

Dolce & Gabbana Gold-tone, Swarovski crystal and faux pearl hair slide, $2,995, net-a-porter.com

14

Crown Jewel

There's no such thing as an understated crown, but this crown-headband hybrid is easy enough to wear to a dinner party or festive night out.

Erickson Beamon

Princess rose gold-plated crystal headband, $930,net-a-porter.com

15

Rodarte

Hairstylist Odile Gilbert and her team spent hours cutting and sewing excess fabric from the Rodarte collection onto headbands, pins, and barrettes. Placed randomly throughout the hair, the scraps looked like ethereal veils.

16

Spike It

There's no such thing as looking too twee when you're wearing a headband covered with spikes (and pearls).
