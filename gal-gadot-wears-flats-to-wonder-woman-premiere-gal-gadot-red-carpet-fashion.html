Gal Gadot Wears Flats to 'Wonder Woman' Premiere - Gal Gadot Red Carpet Fashion

September 25, 2024
blog

High heels are par for the course on the red carpet. Gal Gadot is not about that life. At last night's Hollywood premiere of Wonder Woman, the star opted for a $50 pair of gold thong sandals from Aldo instead.

She wore them under a red, sequined Givenchy gown.

When asked on the red carpet about why she chose flats, she had an easy answer: "It's more comfortable! That's it."

.@GalGadot has a very simple reason for wearing flats instead of heels to the #WonderWoman premiere. pic.twitter.com/P6ytSnjOIuMay 26, 2017

In fact, Gal has been committed to wearing flats throughout her press tour.

Hero, indeed.
