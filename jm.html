JM

July 20, 2024
blog

We're amped to premiere two new cuts from Detroit rapper Prada Leary, which he's calling the Zag 4 Essentials. The first, "Iceberg Slim Pt. 2," featured Maxo Kream and was produced by Dag and IcePic. The second Prada banger of the day is a solo cut produced by 808 Mafia and Kase Work: "JM." 

Prada has an icy sound that gives listeners a fleeting sense of Detroit, but due to his energy, and his embrace of eclectic trap production, he's earned the attention of Southern producers like 808 Mafia. "JM" is an abrasive number that throws you right into the vortex of an adrenaline-rushing night at the trap house. The content is familiar, but Leary brings a raw energy that'll have you coming back for more.
