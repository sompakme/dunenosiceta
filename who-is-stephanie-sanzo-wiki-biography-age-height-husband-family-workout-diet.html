Early Life and Background

Stephanie Sanzo was born on April 2, 1987, in Australia. She grew up in Geelong, Victoria, with her sister Katie Sanzo, and her parents Nicole Sanzo and Steph Sanzaro. She suffered from pelvic prolapse after her delivery, which prevented her from doing any high-impact exercises like running. She switched to weight training instead and fell in love with lifting heavy weights. She also started following a healthy diet and eating organic foods.

Workout Plan

  • Phase 1: Foundation – This phase focuses on building a solid base of strength and technique, using compound exercises and moderate weights.
  • Phase 2: Strength – This phase increases the intensity and volume of the workouts, using heavier weights and lower reps.
  • Phase 3: Power – This phase introduces explosive movements and plyometrics, using lighter weights and higher reps.
  • Phase 4: Peak – This phase combines all the elements of the previous phases, using a variety of weights, reps, and exercises.

Each workout session lasts for about 45 minutes, and consists of three parts:

  • Activation – This part involves warming up the muscles and joints with some light exercises, such as band walks, glute bridges, or shoulder rotations.
  • Main Workout – This part involves performing three to four sets of four to six exercises, targeting different muscle groups. The exercises include squats, deadlifts, lunges, bench presses, rows, pull-ups, curls, triceps extensions, and more.
  • Finisher – This part involves doing one or two sets of one or two exercises, using high reps and low rest periods. The exercises include burpees, mountain climbers, jump squats, or push-ups.
