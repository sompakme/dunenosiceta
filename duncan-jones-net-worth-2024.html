Early Life and Career Beginnings

Duncan Jones was born as Duncan Zowie Haywood Jones in Beckenham, London. He was the first child of David Bowie and his first wife, Angie. Jones spent his early years immersed in a creative environment, which undoubtedly influenced his future career path. He studied philosophy at the College of Wooster in Ohio and later graduated from the prestigious London Film School. His early career saw him working on commercials and as a director for the music video of his father’s song “Little Wonder.”

Breakthrough in Film Industry

Jones’ breakthrough came with his directorial debut, “Moon,” in 2009. Subsequent Film Projects
